Mac and Cheese Recipe

This recipe is cheesy and delicous. Its also easy to make and budget friendly. if you are looking for a good meal to make that is easy and feeds the whole family, here you go!

Recipe

  • 1 box of Macaroni
  • 1 block of cheddar cheese
  • 6 cups of shredded Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 pint of heavy cream or whole milk
  • 1 tbs Butter
  • 1 Tbs of all purpose-flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350째F (175째C)
  2. In a large pot, boil the Macaroni for about 7-10 minutes, until al dente or desired tenderness.
  3. Take your cheddar cheese blocks and cut them into 1 inch cubes. Save for later.
  4. In a large sauce pan, make the rue: this is done by melting equal parts butter and equal parts flour.
    • Take 1Tbs of butter and 1 Tbps flour.Place butter into sauce pan
    • as the butter is almost done melting slowly stir in the flour until you get a thicken mixture.
    • Once the mixture has thickened and is fully incorporated, add in your milk / heavy cream.
  5. Now that your rue is done, you may now add in your cheddar cheese cubes that you cut from earlier. Stir until completely melted.
  6. Once melted, you may add your shredded cheese to get the consistency that you want. For this recipe, you may add 2 cups of shredded cheese. Should be a thick but smooth texture.
  7. Add your cheese sauce to your cooked Macaroni and stuff until fully mixed.
  8. In a baking pan,add a layer of the mixed mac and cheese and spread over the surface of the pan.
  9. Take your shredded cheese and cover the top of the layer you just spread out. Repeat this until you are done with your mac and cheese mixture. Finish the layer with the rest of the shredded cheese that you have left over.
  10. Once you are done, throw your mac and cheese in the oven and bake for 20-30 minutes at 350째F (175째C).

Thats it! You are done. Enjoy!
